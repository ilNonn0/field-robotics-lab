# Field Robotics and Perception Lab

**Istituto di Intelligenza Meccanica — Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, Pisa**

This repository hosts the landing page of the **Field Robotics and Perception Lab**, a research laboratory focused on autonomous aerial and terrestrial robotics for inspection, exploration, and environmental monitoring.

🌐 **Live site:** [ilNonn0.github.io/field-robotics-lab](https://ilNonn0.github.io/field-robotics-lab)

---

## Research pillars

1. **Autonomous Exploration & SLAM** — lightweight SLAM and GNN-based planners for unknown, GNSS-denied environments
2. **Heterogeneous Multi-Agent Cooperation** — coordinated UAV + UGV missions, task allocation, decentralised decisions
3. **Infrastructure Inspection** — autonomous MAV missions in confined spaces and civil structures
4. **Environmental Monitoring** — aerial and ground platforms for large-scale periodic monitoring

## Infrastructure

- VICON Vero motion capture arena (10 cameras, 4×6×3 m, 1 mm accuracy, 333 Hz)
- Custom 7" autonomous quadrotors with x86 companion computers
- Drone X500 with LiDAR, thermal camera, stereo vision, Intel NUC
- Edge inference UAV with NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX
- Clearpath Husky A200 UGV + Unitree Go2 EDU Plus quadruped
- Kinova Gen3 7DoF arm for mobile manipulation
